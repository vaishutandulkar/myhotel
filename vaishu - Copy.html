About


A Beachside Sanctuary in Morjim


crowned the habitat of Olive Ridley sea turtles and a plethora
of birds, Morjim is a charming town in the state of Goa. It is known to
attract locals as well as tourists from around the globe, thanks to its pristine
beaches, serene surroundings and ecstatic nightlife. Its placement in North
Goa cannot be any more perfect. Morjim is glorified by the exquisite restaurants, shacks,
bars, riveting beaches and a variety of other scenic landmarks in
the area, like Morjim Beach, Ashwem Beach, Burger Factory
and Chapora Fort. If you are visiting this spirited side of Goa, Stone Wood Village
Resort in Morjim welcomes you to an indelible beachside stay. Ensconced
in comfort, in the lap of lush greenery and nature, guests are treated to
well-appointed rooms that give way to brilliant views of the beach and the surrounding
lawn. Beautifully decorated with an outdoor swimming pool and lush green gardens,
our Morjim resort enables you to bask in all the glory of beach life! Note: The swimming pool of Stone Wood Village Resort, Morjim will
be under maintenance from 2nd May to 8th May 2023.
