A Dining Affair to Remember

A quaint multi-cuisine restaurant in Morjim, Village Cafe delivers lip-smacking Goan delicacies and alcoholic drinks. The diner provides a la carte as well as buffet options for breakfast, lunch and dinner. It aims to create a carefree ambience where guests can indulge in an unforgettable gastronomical experience in the company of their loved ones. From candlelight dinners to friends catching up, our restaurant in Goa curates the perfect meal for all types of occasions. The restaurant is functional between 8:30 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.
