Beachside Accommodations at Stone Wood Village Resort

GARDEN VIEW COLLAGE

Spread across an area of 144 sq. ft., the Garden View Cottages in Morjim
deliver a cosy yet opulent stay. The cottages overlook mesmerising views
of the surrounding nature and the garden. There are a total of 15 rooms.
The wooden interiors really do enhance the aesthetics of these rooms.



POOL FRONT COTTAGE

As the name suggests, the Pool Front Cottages in Goa overlook the
turquoise swimming pool. The 144 sq. ft. Morjim cottages can accommodate
up to 2 adults. There are a total of 10 rooms under this category. The
rustic appearance magnified by the wooden interiors and decors amplify
the overall living experience.
